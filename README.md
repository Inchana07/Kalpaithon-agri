DIRECT FARMERS TO CONSUMER MARKET SPACE
📌 Problem Statement :
Smallholder farmers suffer significant financial losses and waste due to a lack of market visibility and negotiation power against local middlemen.

📝 Description :
The current agricultural supply chain in Mandya is fractured by information asymmetry. Smallholder farmers grow crops with no data on who wants to buy them or at what price, leaving them vulnerable to "distress sales" where they sell at a loss just to avoid total spoilage. Local middlemen exploit this lack of transparency, taking a massive cut of the profit while providing little value to the actual producer. This system discourages youth from entering agriculture and keeps rural farming communities in a cycle of debt.

Solution :
Our solution is a hyperlocal D2C web platform designed to eliminate the middleman and return fair profits to smallholder farmers. By connecting farms directly to families within a 100km radius, we replace opaque supply chains with transparent, real-time transactions.
Key innovations include WhatsApp/SMS integration for accessibility on basic smartphones, shelf-life management alerts to reduce spoilage, and a flash-sale feature for bulk inventory. The process is frictionless: farmers list their produce, nearby consumers discover fresh stock, and orders are settled via WhatsApp for direct delivery. This ecosystem ensures higher farmer earnings, reduced food waste, and fresher produce for consumers.

Core Technology Stack
Backend: Python with Flask Framework
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
Database:SQLite (managed via `market.db`)
Key Integrations: Planned WhatsApp/SMS gateway for real-time notifications
